Frequently asked questions

Everything about converting HEIC images.

What is a HEIC file and why convert it?

HEIC (High Efficiency Image Container) is the default photo format on iPhone and iPad. It saves storage space but isn’t supported by many apps, websites, and Windows programs. Converting HEIC to JPG or PNG makes your photos open everywhere.

Is this HEIC image converter free?

Yes — it’s completely free with no limits, no watermark, and no signup required.

Are my photos uploaded to a server?

No. Image conversion happens locally in your browser using JavaScript, so your selected photos are not uploaded for processing.

Can I convert multiple HEIC images at once?

Yes. Drag and drop many HEIC files, convert them in a batch, and download them together as a ZIP file.

Should I convert HEIC to JPG or PNG?

Choose JPG for photos and smaller files, and PNG when you need lossless quality or transparency. For sharing photos, JPG is usually the best choice.

Does it work on iPhone and Android?

Yes. The converter runs in any modern mobile or desktop browser, including Safari on iPhone and Chrome on Android.
